Fruit Salad

This Fruit Salad Recipe is sure to be a hit at any food gathering!

This recipe has been in my family for years and years. We make it at every holiday family get together and everyone loves it! It's a great refreshing fruit salad for every occasion, especially summer months and hot days.

Ingredients

  • Dole Pineapple Tidbits
  • Dole Mandarin Oranges
  • Red Seedless Grapes (Halved)
  • Small Marshmallows
  • Sour Cream

Steps

  1. Thouroghly drain the pineapple and mandarin orages and then add them in large mixing bowl
  2. Wash and cut the Red Seedless Grapes and combine in mixing bowl
  3. Add Sour Cream and Marshmallows in mixing bowl
  4. Stir well, making sure to combine sour cream
  5. Refrigerate for a few hours before serving for best results
